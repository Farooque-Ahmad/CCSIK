Vanmanus: Principal Sir ka Ek Anokha Tohfa

School ke woh din, jab hum sab doston ka ek hi mission hota tha—masti aur shaitani! Aur tabhi Principal Sir ne hume ek naam diya jo kabhi na bhoolne wala hai—“Vanmanus”!

Sir ka andaaz hi alag tha—gussa karte, par uske peeche pyar chhupa hota tha. Jab class ka shor unka sabr tod deta, toh woh hamesha haste hue kehte, “Tum log toh bilkul Vanmanus ho!” Poora class chup ho jata, lekin unke jaane ke baad hasi rukti nahi thi

Unka “Vanmanus” kehna humare liye medal tha. Kabhi punish karte, kabhi pyar se samjhate, par unka yeh shabd ek yaad ban gaya. Aaj lagta hai, yeh naam unka pyar-bhara tareef tha. Sir, agar aap yeh sun rahe hain, toh hum aaj bhi woh shaitani karne wale Vanmanus hain—bas ab aapki muskaan aur pyar ki bohot yaad aati hai!
